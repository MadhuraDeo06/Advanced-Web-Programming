  3. Damon salvatore

    • Favourite Dialouge : If it meant that I got to be with her, have children, grow old with her, if it meant we'd die together, then yes I'd take the cure.

    • Character Information :

      Damon Salvatore is a vampire, turned by Katherine Pierce 145 years prior to the series' debut. He is the son of late Giuseppe Salvatore, ripper Lily Salvatore and older brother of Stefan Salvatore. Damon is portrayed as charming but cruel, taking pleasure in killing and feeding on humans, while Stefan satisfies his vampiric hunger with blood stolen from blood banks.
